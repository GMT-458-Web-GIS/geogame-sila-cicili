This is a spatial analysis simulation. Your goal is to identify the crime zone where the data logically intersects.

  1. Click the EVIDENCE CARDS at the bottom of the screen to activate the color layers on the map (to analyze the data).
  2. Hover over a province to analyze its raw data in the cards.
  3. After examining the mission and evidence, click your correct guess.
  4. You lose 1 life if you make a wrong guess or fail to complete the case within the given time. When you run out of lives, the mission ends.

Before starting, make sure you examine all evidence at least once (Education, Poverty, Prison).
